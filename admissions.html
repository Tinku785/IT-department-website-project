Admissions

Admission Process

Admissions to the Department of Information Technology are based on merit and entrance examinations. We follow a transparent and fair admission process to ensure the best candidates join our programs.

Step 1: Application

Submit your application through the official Gauhati University admission portal or our department's online application system.

Step 2: Entrance Examination

Appear for the relevant entrance examination (JEE for B.Tech, GATE for M.Tech, or university-specific tests).

Step 3: Counseling

Participate in the counseling session where seats are allocated based on merit and preferences.

Step 4: Document Verification

Complete document verification and fee payment to confirm your admission.
