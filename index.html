About Us

The Department of Information Technology at Gauhati University was established in 2012 to provide quality education in computer science and IT. We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs, focusing on software development, data science, AI, and more. Our mission is to produce skilled professionals who contribute to technological advancement.

Key highlights: State-of-the-art labs, industry collaborations, and research in emerging fields.
