Examinations & Results

Examination Schedule

Semester Examinations

Regular semester examinations are conducted twice a year for all undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Key Dates:
  • Odd Semester: November-December
  • Even Semester: April-May
  • Practical Examinations: Following theory exams
Regular Schedule CBCS Pattern
Special Examinations

Special examinations are conducted for backlog papers, improvement examinations, and other special cases.

Types:
  • Backlog Examinations
  • Improvement Examinations
  • Supplementary Examinations
  • Special Carry-over Examinations
On-Demand Flexible Schedule

Results & Grading System

Grading System

The department follows a Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) with absolute grading for fair evaluation.

Grade Points:
  • O (Outstanding): 10
  • A+ (Excellent): 9
  • A (Very Good): 8
  • B+ (Good): 7
  • B (Above Average): 6
  • C (Average): 5
  • P (Pass): 4
  • F (Fail): 0
CBCS System Absolute Grading
Result Declaration

Results are declared within 30-45 days from the completion of examinations. Students can access results through the university portal.

Process:
  • Answer script evaluation
  • Result compilation and verification
  • Online result publication
  • Re-evaluation and scrutiny options
