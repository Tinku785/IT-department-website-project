About the Department

Our History

The Department of Information Technology at Gauhati University was established in 2012 to provide quality education in computer science and IT. Since its inception, the department has been at the forefront of technological education in Northeast India.

Over the years, we have evolved from a small department to a center of excellence, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Information Technology.

Our Mission

To produce skilled professionals who contribute to technological advancement and innovation. We aim to:

  • Provide world-class education in Information Technology
  • Foster research and development in emerging technologies
  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry
  • Contribute to the digital transformation of society
Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in Information Technology education, research, and innovation in India, recognized globally for our contributions to the field.
